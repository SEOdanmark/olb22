Frequently Asked Questions

Why do people need dental care?

Dental care is essential for maintaining oral health, preventing tooth decay, gum disease, and other oral health issues, as well as preserving overall well-being.

Is oral care self-care?

Yes, oral care involves personal hygiene practices such as brushing, flossing, and using mouthwash to maintain oral health. It is an integral aspect of self-care routines.

What is the golden rule for oral hygiene?

The golden rule for oral hygiene is to brush your teeth at least twice a day, floss daily, maintain a balanced diet, limit sugary snacks and drinks, and visit your dentist regularly for check-ups and cleanings.

How to clean gums?

Cleaning gums involves gentle brushing along the gumline, using a soft-bristled toothbrush or a gum stimulator, and rinsing with an antimicrobial mouthwash to remove plaque and bacteria.
