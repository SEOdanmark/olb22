Frequently Asked Questions

How to do oral care on a patient?

Oral care for patients involves proper techniques for brushing and flossing, providing guidance on diet choices, and scheduling regular dental check-ups and cleanings.

What is the difference between oral health care and mouth care?

Oral health care encompasses a broader range of practices aimed at maintaining the health of teeth, gums, and the entire oral cavity, while mouth care may refer more specifically to hygiene practices for the mouth.

Is oral health a problem?

Oral health issues such as tooth decay, gum disease, and oral cancer can indeed pose significant problems if left untreated. Regular dental care is crucial for preventing and addressing these issues.

Why is it called dental?

"Dental" originates from the Latin word "dens," meaning tooth. It pertains to the branch of medicine focused on the diagnosis, prevention, and treatment of oral diseases and conditions.
